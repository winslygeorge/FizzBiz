Description {{{auth}}}

Young Hustle Competition is the first Fizzbiz network Competition ever. This competition is for those with small businesses who are aspiring to grow their business. The competition is aimed at empowering competitors to be innovative, test and improve their marketing skills and managerial skills.

During the competition, you will be required to grow your business followers, likes , orders and increase the interactions between your services and products with your customers. The competitors with most followers, likes, orders and interactions according to the competition phases rules will be declared winners. Read more about the competition here

Requirements

  • Located in Kenya.
  • Photo copy of National Identification Card (ID) or Driving Lincense or Password.
  • Have a Fizzbiz account.

Note: We encourage competitors to follow and be active in our social media platforms to earn extra points.

Rules

  • A competitor can only register one business account for the competition.
  • A business account can only be registered by one competitor.
  • The competition will count for real active followers and likes only. Detection of any fraud and illegal ways of gaining followers, likes and orders will lead to immediate elimination from the competition.
  • Detection of false orders and checkout will also lead to immediate elimination from the competiton.
Note: We encourage competitors to be honest.

Schedule

  • Entrance/ Registration Phase - July 1st 2022 to September 30th 2022
  • First Selection Phase - October 1st 2022 to October 30th 2022
  • Selection Phase - November 1st 2022 to November 30th 2022
  • Winners Declaration Phase - December 1st 2022

Entrance Phase Description

In this phase we allow everyone who has a business account with Fizzbiz network to join the competition. There is no age limitation to join. After being entered to the competition we encourage the competitors to gather and gain more followers, like and try to have many orders as possible as these are the criterial that we will be using to move forward the successful competitors.
This can be achieved as Fizzbiz network allows business owners to share their profiles with their social media links.

First selection Phase Description

To be selected for the first selection, any competitors who would have gained 1000 Followers, 500 Likes and had received and checked out at least 20 orders will be moved forward with the competition. Your Fizzbiz business account should also have an active platform.

Second Selection Phase Description

To be selected for the second selection, any competitors who would have gained 2000 Followers, 1000 Likes and had received and checked out at least 30 orders will be moved forward with the competition. Your Fizzbiz business account should also have an active platform.

Winners and Prizes

To be selected for the second selection, any competitors who would have gained 2000 Followers, 1000 Likes and had received and checked out at least 30 orders will be moved forward with the competition. Your Fizzbiz business account should also have an active platform.
